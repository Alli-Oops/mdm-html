My Design Marketplace

Revolutionizing the Interior Design Industry

Founder KELLI WALTON (Interior Designer) identified a strong need in the Interior Design industry & My Design Marketplace is the solution. MDM is an iOS app designed to give Interior Designers immediate access to local products.

Business Concepts

  • Returning design items isn't always feasible
  • Selling excess items alleviates storage demands
  • Exclusive access to shop designers' unique inventory
  • Find immediate and locally available items
    • Saves the cost and wait time for shipping

The Need for the App

  • Nothing like this exists
  • Local design items that meet industry standards
    • Enables steeper mark ups to clients
    • Incentive to check MDM first
  • Increases Vendors efficiency
